# Research Toolkit

> **Standardized research methodology for prediction, hypothesis testing, and decision-making under uncertainty.**
>
> Distilled from a multi-cycle high-randomness prediction research project into a domain-agnostic toolkit. Use it for personal investing, health experiments, product A/B testing, career decisions, or any field where you have a hypothesis you want to be true.

[**📖 English version**](en/README.md) — full toolkit in English

## Philosophy in one line

> **The default is no edge. You must prove edge exists. If you cannot, be honest about it — and pivot to a different value that is real.**

> **Mặc định là không có edge. Bạn phải chứng minh có. Nếu không chứng minh được, hãy trung thực về điều đó — và chuyển sang giá trị khác mà vẫn có thật.**
